## ?? Complete Workflow

### Step 1: Faculty Sends Meeting Invitation
Faculty clicks "Send Meeting Invite" from their dashboard:
- Title: "Project Review Meeting"
- Date/Time: Jan 25, 2025 02:00 PM
- Location: Room 301
- Agenda: "Discuss your project progress"

### Step 2: Student Sees Invitation
Student navigates to **Status Update** page:
- Sees "Scheduled Meetings" section at the top
- Meeting card shows with ?? **Pending** status
- Two buttons: **Accept** | **Decline**

### Step 3: Student Accepts Invitation
Student clicks **"Accept"**:
- Success message: "You have accepted the meeting invitation"
- Card status changes to ?? **Accepted**
- New button appears: **"Mark as Attended"**

### Step 4: After Attending the Meeting
Student clicks **"Mark as Attended"**:
- If **both students** mark as attended:
  - ? **Automatically creates Meeting #5** in the Project Progress table
  - Uses meeting title and description as notes
  - Sets completion % to last meeting's value (student can edit later)
  - Status changes to ?? **Attended**
  - Success message: "Meeting marked as attended and added to your project progress!"
- If only one student marks:
  - Success message: "Waiting for your teammate to mark attendance"

### Step 5: Meeting Appears in Progress Table
Now the meeting appears in the **Project Progress Details** table:
- **Meeting #5**
- **Date**: Jan 25, 2025
- **Completion**: 15% (copied from Meeting #4)
- **Notes**: "Project Review Meeting - Discuss your project progress"
- **Proof**: None (can upload later via Edit)
- Student can click **Edit** to update notes, proof, completion %
